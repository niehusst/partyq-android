Build your queue

What's a party without music? But managing song requests all night is boring. With partyq (and Spotify Premium), you can host a music queue that you and your friends can all add songs to whenever you want.

Share with friends

Sharing a music queue with friends has never been easier. You and your friends choose which songs to play, partyq does the rest.

All your favorite songs from Spotify

The partyq app uses Spotify to find and play music. That means that you can access all your favorite songs on partyq.
